Oshwsp-gaitdataset0

Small dataset testing the OSHWSP platform for gait analysis.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Description

This dataset includes the sampling data of twelve subjects: six female and six male. Each subject was asked to walk at his or her own preferred speed through a straight course in two occasions under the same conditions. The length of the course was approximately 20 meters.

The platform used to acquire the data is available at http://percomp.github.io/oshwsp
